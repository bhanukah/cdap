Lawbot

A Community Based Chatbot to Provide Legal Assistance

Welcome to Lawbot

Lawbot is a legal assistant chatbot which helps people with their legal issues. So far, there are no any legal assistant chatbots in Sri Lanka which have been made specifically for Sri Lankan laws. Lawbot will be the first Sri-Lankan legal assistant chatbot.

Today lot of people face the question of from where and whom to find the legal assistance for the different issues they face in life. To find the accurate and contemporary legal solutions, most people need to go through lot of hassles wasting lot of their time, money and effort. To overcome this issue, we have come up with Lawbot.

At the moment, Lawbot will be made only for three legal areas which were finalised through results of a survey we have done for three months. Those areas are ‘Road Accidents’, ‘Citizen's Registrations’ and ‘Employment Information’.

How Lawbot differs from existing legislation related chatbots

1. Lawbot is more conversation based unlike most available chatbots which are structure based.

2. Provides answers for the legal issues promptly, unlike existing chatbots which ask you to wait till an attorney gets back to you.

3. All the solutions provided are free of charge, unlike existing chatbots which are made specifically for law firms.

4. Lawbot will be available all 24/7.

5. Supports voice as well.

6. If there are any documents involved in, related to the user’s question, Lawbot has the ability to let the user to download those documents.

7. If user requests for the directions to a certain legal place related to his question, Lawbot will provide the user with the directions to the nearby places.
